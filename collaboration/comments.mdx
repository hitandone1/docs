Comments let your team leave feedback, ask questions, and discuss content directly inside a page — without modifying the underlying text. Every comment is threaded, searchable, and tied to the exact block or text selection it references, so context is never lost.

## Adding a Comment

Jetie supports two ways to start a comment thread: by selecting text or by targeting a whole block.

### Comment on Selected Text

1. Highlight any text on the page.
2. A floating toolbar appears above your selection — click the **comment bubble** icon (💬).
3. Type your comment in the input box.
4. Press **Cmd + Enter** (Mac) or **Ctrl + Enter** (Windows/Linux) to submit, or click the **Submit** button.

The selected text becomes highlighted, and a comment indicator appears in the page margin.

### Comment on a Block

1. Hover over any block (paragraph, heading, image, etc.).
2. Click the **+** icon that appears to the left of the block.
3. Select **Comment** from the block menu.
4. Type and submit your comment.

<Tip>
  Resolve comment threads as soon as they're actioned to keep your pages clean and focused. Teammates scanning the page will instantly see which discussions are still open versus settled.
</Tip>

## Inline vs. Page-Level Comments

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Inline Comments">
    Inline comments are anchored to a specific text selection or block. They appear as **highlights** directly in the page body, with the full thread accessible in the right-side **comments panel**. Click any highlight to expand the thread.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Page-Level Comments">
    Page-level comments are not attached to any specific block. To view or add them, scroll to the **bottom of the page** and find the page comment thread. This is a great place for high-level feedback or general discussion about the document as a whole.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## @Mentioning Teammates

Type `@` followed by a teammate's name inside any comment to send them a direct notification.

- The mentioned person receives both an **in-app notification** (the bell icon in the top-right) and an **email notification**.
- You can mention multiple people in a single comment by typing `@` for each name.
- Mentions also work in comment **replies**.

<Note>
  You can only @mention people who are workspace members or who have been given access to the page. Typing an external email address after `@` will not trigger a notification.
</Note>

## Replying to a Comment

1. Find the comment thread you want to respond to — either in the page margin or in the comments panel.
2. Click **Reply** beneath the last message in the thread.
3. Type your reply and press **Cmd + Enter** or **Ctrl + Enter** to post it.

All replies are nested inside the original thread, keeping related discussion together.

## Reacting to a Comment

1. Hover over any individual comment in a thread.
2. Click the **emoji icon** (🙂) that appears on the right side.
3. Select an emoji from the picker.

Reactions are visible to everyone with access to the page. Multiple people can add the same reaction, and the count increments next to the emoji.

## Resolving Comments

When a discussion has been addressed, mark it resolved to archive the thread:

1. Open the comment thread.
2. Click the **checkmark** (✓) icon at the top-right of the thread.
3. The thread collapses and moves to the **Resolved** section of the comments panel.

Resolved comments are never deleted — you can search and view them at any time by clicking **Show resolved** in the comments panel.

## Editing and Deleting Your Own Comments

1. Hover over your comment to reveal the **three-dot menu** (⋯).
2. Select **Edit** to modify the text, or **Delete** to remove it.

<Warning>
  Deleting a comment also removes all replies in that thread. This action cannot be undone. If you only need to correct a typo, use **Edit** instead.
</Warning>

## Managing Comment Notifications

Control how and when Jetie notifies you about new comments:

1. Click your avatar in the bottom-left corner and select **Profile & Settings**.
2. Navigate to **Settings → Notifications → Comments**.
3. Choose your preferences:

| Notification type | Options |
|---|---|
| In-app notifications | On / Off |
| Email — immediate | On / Off (sent on every new comment or mention) |
| Email digest | Daily / Weekly / Off |
| Desktop push | On / Off (requires browser permission) |

<Tip>
  If you're being notified about comments on pages you no longer actively work on, open the page, click **…** → **Unfollow**, and you'll stop receiving notifications for that page specifically.
</Tip>
