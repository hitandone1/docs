Jetie is your all-in-one productivity workspace — think Notion-style pages and databases, Confluence-level PDF handling, and a built-in AI assistant that doesn't just answer questions, it breaks down your goals into actionable tasks and works alongside you. Whether you're managing a project, writing a spec, or exploring a complex topic, Jetie keeps everything in one place.

## Get started in minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [app.jetie.app](https://app.jetie.app/signup) — no credit card required for the free plan. You can invite teammates during signup or any time afterward.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your workspace">
    Choose a workspace name, pick a template (blank, project tracker, or knowledge base), and configure your first pages. See [Workspace Setup](/workspace-setup) for details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write your first page">
    Open the editor and start typing. Use `/` to insert blocks — headings, to-dos, images, tables, and more. Your content auto-saves as you type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Chat with the AI">
    Click the **Ask AI** button in any page or press `Cmd/Ctrl + K` to open the AI chat. Describe a goal and let Jetie decompose it into tasks automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>
