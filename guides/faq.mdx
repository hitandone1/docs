## Getting Started

<Accordion title="Is there a free plan?">
  Yes. Jetie's free plan includes one workspace, 50 AI interactions per month, and full access to all core editing features — including block-based documents, databases, kanban boards, and page templates. The free plan has no time limit; it remains free as long as you stay within the usage limits.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do I need a credit card to sign up?">
  No. You can create a Jetie account and start using the free plan without providing any payment information. A credit card is only required when you upgrade to a paid plan.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I import my Notion or Confluence content?">
  Yes. Go to **Settings → Import**, select **Notion** or **Confluence**, and follow the OAuth authentication flow. Jetie maps your existing page hierarchy, block types, and database structures automatically. After the import completes, your pages appear in the workspace exactly as they were organized in the source tool. Review the imported pages and apply any Jetie-specific formatting you want.
</Accordion>

## Documents & Editor

<Accordion title="Does Jetie autosave?">
  Yes. Every change you make is saved in real time as you type — there is no Save button, and you will never lose work by closing a tab or navigating away. Jetie also maintains a full version history for every page so you can review or restore any previous state.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I see the edit history for a page?">
  Yes. Open the page options menu by clicking **…** in the top-right corner of any page and select **Page history**. You'll see a timestamped list of every version, with a preview of the content at each point. Click any version to view it in full, and click **Restore this version** to roll back the page to that state.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What file types can I embed in a page?">
  Jetie supports a wide range of embeds:

  - **Images** — PNG, JPG, GIF, WebP
  - **Videos** — MP4, MOV (uploaded directly or embedded from YouTube, Vimeo, and Loom)
  - **PDFs** — rendered inline with a scrollable viewer
  - **Audio** — MP3, WAV, OGG
  - **External embeds** — YouTube, Figma, Google Maps, Miro, Airtable, and any site that supports oEmbed or iframe embedding

  To embed any of these, type `/embed` in the editor and choose the type you need.
</Accordion>

## AI Features

<Accordion title="Is my data used to train AI models?">
  No. Jetie processes your content to answer queries and generate suggestions within your session, but your data is never used to train AI models — not Jetie's models and not any third-party model provider's training datasets. Your workspace content remains private to your organization. See the [Jetie Privacy Policy](#) for the full details.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I turn off AI features?">
  Yes. You can enable or disable each AI feature independently. Go to **Settings → AI** and toggle any of the following on or off:

  - **Inline suggestions** — AI-powered text completions that appear as you type
  - **Context Memory** — the AI's ability to remember information from previous sessions
  - **AI Chat** — the full AI assistant panel

  Turning off all three disables AI entirely for your account. Workspace admins can also disable AI features at the workspace level for all members.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Why did the AI give an incorrect answer?">
  AI assistants can make mistakes, especially on questions that require precise factual information, recent data, or nuanced judgment. If you receive an answer that seems wrong or incomplete, always verify it against a primary source before acting on it. To help Jetie improve, click the **thumbs-down** icon below the response and optionally add a brief note explaining what was wrong. Your feedback is reviewed by the Jetie team and used to improve response quality — not to train models on your data.
</Accordion>

## Collaboration

<Accordion title="How many people can edit a page at the same time?">
  The number of simultaneous editors depends on your plan:

  | Plan | Simultaneous Editors |
  |---|---|
  | Free | Up to 3 |
  | Pro | Up to 10 |
  | Business | Up to 50 |

  All collaborators see each other's cursors and changes in real time. If your team regularly needs more than 50 simultaneous editors, contact [Jetie sales](#) to discuss an Enterprise arrangement.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can guests comment on a page without being able to edit it?">
  Yes. When you share a page with an external guest, set their access level to **Can comment**. Guests with this role can read the full page and leave inline comments on any text, but they cannot modify the page content. This is the recommended setting for client reviews, stakeholder feedback rounds, and contract reviews.
</Accordion>

## Billing

<Accordion title="What happens to my data if I cancel my plan?">
  If you cancel a paid plan, your workspace downgrades to the free tier rather than being deleted immediately. Your data — pages, databases, files, and settings — is retained for **90 days** after cancellation. During that window you can export everything by going to **Settings → Export → Export all content**. After 90 days, data is permanently deleted in accordance with Jetie's data retention policy.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I switch from monthly to annual billing?">
  Yes. Go to **Settings → Billing → Billing cycle** and select **Annual**. When you switch, Jetie calculates a credit for the unused portion of your current monthly period and applies it against your first annual invoice. Annual billing offers a discount compared to monthly billing — the exact percentage is shown on the billing page before you confirm the switch.
</Accordion>
