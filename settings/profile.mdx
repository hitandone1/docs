Your Jetie profile controls how you appear to teammates across every workspace you belong to. It also houses personal preferences — like theme, font size, and notifications — that apply only to your account and do not affect anyone else on your team.

## Accessing Profile Settings

Click your **avatar** in the bottom-left corner of any Jetie page, then select **Profile & Settings**. This opens your personal settings panel, separate from workspace-level settings.

## Profile Settings

The **Profile** tab lets you control your public-facing identity inside Jetie:

- **Display name** — The name shown on your comments, cursor label, and member list. Use your real name or a consistent handle your teammates will recognise.
- **Profile photo** — Upload a JPEG, PNG, or WebP image. Recommended size is 256 × 256 px. Your photo appears next to every comment and mention you make.
- **Bio** — A short description visible on your profile card when teammates hover over your name.
- **Preferred pronouns** — Displayed alongside your name on your profile card. Choose from the provided options or enter a custom value.

## Account Settings

The **Account** tab manages your login credentials and connected sign-in methods:

- **Email address** — The address used for notifications and login. See the FAQ below for instructions on changing it.
- **Password** — Click **Change password** to set a new one. You'll need to enter your current password first.
- **Connected login methods** — Link or unlink **Google** or **GitHub SSO** sign-in. You can have multiple methods connected simultaneously. At least one active login method is required at all times.

## Appearance

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Theme">
    Navigate to **Settings → Appearance** and choose one of three theme modes:

    - **Light** — White background with dark text. Best for well-lit environments.
    - **Dark** — Dark background with light text. Reduces eye strain in low-light settings.
    - **System default** — Jetie automatically matches your operating system's current theme and switches with it.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Font Size">
    Adjust the base font size used across all pages:

    - **Small** — Compact layout; fits more content on screen.
    - **Medium** — The default; comfortable for most displays.
    - **Large** — Increases readability on high-resolution or large monitors.

    Font size changes apply only to your view and do not affect how other users see the page.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Language

Jetie supports **20+ UI languages**. To change your interface language:

1. Go to **Settings → Language**.
2. Select your preferred language from the dropdown list.
3. The interface reloads immediately in the selected language.

<Note>
  Changing your UI language affects menus, tooltips, and system messages only. Page content is not translated automatically — content appears in the language it was written in.
</Note>

## Notification Preferences

Go to **Settings → Notifications** to control how Jetie contacts you:

| Notification type | Options |
|---|---|
| In-app notifications | On / Off |
| Email — immediate | On / Off |
| Email digest | Daily / Weekly / Off |
| Desktop push | On / Off |

Notification categories include comments and mentions, page shares, task assignments, and workspace member activity. You can toggle each category independently.

## Keyboard Shortcuts

Jetie includes a full set of keyboard shortcuts for editing, navigation, and commands. To manage them:

- Go to **Settings → Shortcuts** to see the complete list organised by category.
- Toggle **Enable keyboard shortcuts** at the top of the page to turn all shortcuts on or off globally.

<Tip>
  Press **?** anywhere in Jetie to open the keyboard shortcuts quick-reference panel without leaving your current page.
</Tip>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="How do I change my email address?">
  Go to **Settings → Account** and click the **Edit** icon next to your current email address. Enter your new address and click **Send verification email**. Jetie sends a confirmation link to the new address — click it to complete the change. Until you verify the new address, your original email remains active for login and notifications.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I change my display name?">
  Yes. Go to **Settings → Profile** and click your current display name to edit it. Type the new name and press **Enter** or click **Save**. The change takes effect immediately and updates everywhere your name appears — including past comments and mentions.
</Accordion>

## Deleting Your Account

<Warning>
  Deleting your account is permanent and irreversible. All of your personal settings and preferences are erased. Pages you created in shared workspaces are retained and reassigned to the workspace Owner.
</Warning>

1. Go to **Settings → Account**.
2. Scroll to the bottom and click **Delete account**.
3. In the confirmation dialog, type **delete** exactly as shown.
4. Click **Permanently delete my account**.

If you are the sole Owner of a workspace, you must either transfer ownership to another member or delete the workspace before you can delete your account.
