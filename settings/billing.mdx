Jetie offers a free plan for individuals getting started and paid plans that unlock more AI interactions, larger file storage, advanced collaboration features, and enterprise controls. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time from the Billing settings page.

## Plans Overview

| Feature | Free | Pro | Business |
|---|---|---|---|

| AI interactions / month | 50 | 500 | Unlimited |
| File upload size limit | 5 MB | 1 GB | 1 GB |

<Tip>
  Choosing **annual billing** saves approximately **20%** compared to paying month-to-month. The discount is applied immediately when you switch, and you are charged for a full year upfront. You can switch back to monthly at the end of your annual cycle.
</Tip>

## Upgrading Your Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing settings">
    Click your workspace name in the top-left corner, select **Settings**, then click **Billing** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Upgrade">
    Select **Upgrade** next to the plan you want. A plan comparison summary is shown before you commit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a billing cycle">
    Select **Monthly** or **Annual**. The annual price per user is shown with the savings amount highlighted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter payment information">
    Enter your credit or debit card details. Business plan customers can request invoicing after the first payment — contact support to enable this.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your upgrade">
    Click **Confirm and upgrade**. Your workspace is upgraded instantly. AI interaction limits and storage allowances reset at the start of your new billing cycle.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Billing Cycle and Payment Methods

- **Billing cycles**: Monthly or annual. Your renewal date is shown at the top of the Billing page.
- **Payment methods**: Visa, Mastercard, and American Express credit and debit cards. Business plan customers can request invoice-based billing (net-30 payment terms) through the support team.
- **Receipts**: An email receipt is sent to your account email after every successful charge.

## Managing Seats

Paid plans are priced **per user per month**. To add or remove seats:

- **Adding a member**: Invite them from **Settings → Members**. Billing adjusts **pro-rata** — you are charged only for the remaining days in the current billing period.
- **Removing a member**: Go to **Settings → Members**, click the three-dot menu next to the member, and select **Remove from workspace**. The seat is released immediately and credited pro-rata to your next invoice.

<Note>
  Guests do not consume paid member seats. You are only billed for workspace members with the Owner, Admin, or Member role.
</Note>

## Cancelling Your Subscription

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Cancel plan**.
3. Select a cancellation reason (optional) and click **Confirm cancellation**.

Your workspace remains on the paid plan until the **end of the current billing period**, then reverts to the Free plan automatically. You are not charged again after cancellation.

<Note>
  **Your data is always retained after cancellation.** No pages, files, or history are deleted when you downgrade to Free. If you exceed Free plan limits (for example, you have 8 guests but Free allows 5), existing guests will lose access until you remove the excess or re-upgrade. You can re-upgrade to a paid plan at any time to restore full access.
</Note>

## Downloading Invoices

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Scroll down to **Invoice history**.
3. Click the **Download** icon next to any invoice to save a PDF copy.

Invoices include your workspace name, billing period, seat count, unit price, applicable taxes, and total charged. Business plan customers receiving invoice billing can also download purchase orders from this section.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="What happens to my AI interactions if I don't use them all?">
  AI interaction credits do not roll over. Any unused interactions from the current month expire at the end of the billing cycle and reset to your plan's monthly allowance.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I switch from annual to monthly billing?">
  Yes, but the change takes effect at the end of your current annual cycle — you cannot switch mid-year and receive a refund for unused months. Go to **Settings → Billing → Manage plan** to schedule the switch.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a discount for non-profits or educational institutions?">
  Yes. Jetie offers discounted plans for registered non-profits and accredited educational institutions. Contact the support team with proof of status to apply the discount to your account.
</Accordion>
