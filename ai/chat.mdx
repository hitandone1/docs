Jetie AI Chat lets you have a live conversation with an AI that already knows your workspace. Instead of copying content into a separate tool, you ask questions directly — about your pages, your tasks, your documents — and get answers grounded in what you've actually written. Whether you need a quick summary or a drafted email, AI Chat meets you where your work lives.

## Opening AI Chat

You can start a conversation from anywhere in Jetie using any of the following methods:

- **Chat icon** — click the speech-bubble icon in the bottom-left corner of the sidebar
- **Keyboard shortcut** — press `Cmd+K` on macOS or `Ctrl+K` on Windows and Linux
- **Inline trigger** — click **Ask AI** inside any open page to start a conversation scoped to that page

## Scoping Your Context

Jetie AI Chat reads your workspace content to answer questions accurately. By default, the AI reads the page you currently have open. You can expand or narrow that scope using **@-mentions** in your message.

| Scope | Syntax | What it includes |
|---|---|---|
| Current page | *(default)* | The page you have open |
| Entire workspace | `@workspace` | All indexed pages and tasks |
| Specific page | `@page-name` | Only the named page |

<Tip>
  Use `@workspace` when your question spans multiple projects or documents — for example, "What are all the open action items across `@workspace`?" Jetie searches your entire content index and surfaces the most relevant results.
</Tip>

## Example Prompts

The following prompts give you a sense of what AI Chat can do out of the box:

```text
"Summarize this spec for a non-technical audience"
"What tasks are assigned to me this week?"
"What decisions were made in last week's meeting notes?"
"Draft a follow-up email based on this meeting page"
```

You can follow up on any response — ask for more detail, a different format, or a shorter version, and the AI will adjust without losing the thread of the conversation.

## Inserting AI Output into a Page

When AI Chat returns a response you want to keep, click **Insert into page** at the bottom of that response. Jetie places the content as a new block at your cursor position in the currently open page. You can then edit it like any other block.

## Multi-Turn Conversations

AI Chat remembers the last **20 messages** in a session, so you can refer back to earlier context without repeating yourself. For example, you can ask a follow-up like "Make that shorter" or "Add a section on timelines" and the AI understands what "that" refers to.

To start a completely fresh conversation — with no prior context — click the **broom icon** in the top-right corner of the chat panel.

## Chat History

Every conversation is automatically saved. To revisit a past session:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the sidebar">
    Click the **AI** section in the left sidebar to expand it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Recent Chats">
    Click **Recent chats** to see a list of your saved conversations, ordered by most recent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reopen a conversation">
    Click any conversation to reopen it. You can continue the thread or read back through the history.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Chat history is retained for **30 days**. Conversations older than 30 days are automatically deleted. If you want to preserve important AI-generated content permanently, use **Insert into page** to save it to a workspace page before it expires.
</Note>
