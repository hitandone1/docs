Smart Suggestions bring AI assistance directly into your editing experience. As you write, Jetie watches what you type and quietly offers completions, improvements, and relevant content — surfaced inline so you never have to leave the page. Whether you want to fix a paragraph's clarity, translate a section, or generate a table of contents in one click, Smart Suggestions put those actions within arm's reach.

## Triggering Suggestions

There are three ways to invoke Smart Suggestions depending on what you're trying to do.

### Inline Ghost Text

When you pause while typing, Jetie predicts a continuation and displays it as faded grey text after your cursor. Press `Tab` to accept the suggestion and insert it as live text. Press `Escape` or keep typing to dismiss it.

### AI Actions on Selected Text

Select any text in your document, right-click, and hover over **AI** in the context menu to see the full list of text actions. Each action rewrites or transforms your selection in place.

<Tip>
  You can also select text and press `Cmd+Shift+A` (macOS) or `Ctrl+Shift+A` (Windows/Linux) to open the AI actions menu without reaching for the mouse.
</Tip>

### The `++` Prompt Box

Type `++` at the very start of an empty block and press `Space` to open a floating prompt box. Enter any instruction — "Write a three-sentence introduction for this section" or "Create a comparison table of these two options" — and Jetie generates content directly into the block.

<Tip>
  The `++` shortcut is the fastest way to generate new content in the middle of a document. Unlike AI Chat, it inserts output exactly where your cursor is, so you never need to copy and paste.
</Tip>

## Available AI Actions on Selected Text

| Action | How to trigger | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| **Improve writing** | Select text → right-click → AI → Improve writing | Fixes grammar, tightens phrasing, and improves overall clarity without changing the meaning |
| **Make shorter** | Select text → right-click → AI → Make shorter | Reduces word count while preserving the core message |
| **Make longer** | Select text → right-click → AI → Make longer | Expands the selection with additional supporting detail |
| **Change tone** | Select text → right-click → AI → Change tone | Rewrites the selection in a chosen tone: Professional, Casual, Friendly, or Assertive |
| **Summarize** | Select text → right-click → AI → Summarize | Condenses the selected section into a short summary, inserted as a new block below |
| **Translate** | Select text → right-click → AI → Translate | Translates the selection into any of 30+ supported languages |
| **Explain** | Select text → right-click → AI → Explain | Adds a plain-language explanation of the selected text as a callout block immediately below it |

## Page-Level AI Actions

Beyond inline suggestions, Jetie provides a set of AI actions that operate on the entire page. Find these in the **AI** button in the page toolbar at the top of any document.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Summarize page" icon="align-left">
    Generates a concise summary of the whole page and inserts it at the top as a callout block.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Generate table of contents" icon="list">
    Scans all headings in the page and inserts a linked table of contents block at your cursor position.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Find action items" icon="circle-check">
    Scans the page for sentences that imply tasks or commitments and lists them as a checklist block.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Turning Off Ghost Text

If inline completions interrupt your flow, you can disable them without affecting other Smart Suggestions features.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click your avatar in the bottom-left corner and select **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the AI section">
    In the left panel of Settings, click **AI**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Disable inline completions">
    Under **Inline completions**, toggle the setting to **Off**. Ghost text will no longer appear while you type. All other AI actions — right-click menu, `++` shortcut, and page-level actions — remain available.
  </Step>
</Steps>
