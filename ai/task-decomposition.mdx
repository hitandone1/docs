AI Task Decomposition is Jetie's most powerful feature for turning ambition into action. You describe a goal in plain language — as loosely or as precisely as you like — and the AI generates a structured plan complete with milestones, subtasks, suggested owners, and estimated durations. Rather than staring at a blank project board, you start with a complete scaffold you can immediately review, refine, and adopt.

## How to Decompose a Goal

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open AI Chat">
    Click the chat icon in the bottom-left sidebar, or press `Cmd+K` (macOS) / `Ctrl+K` (Windows/Linux) from anywhere in your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe your goal">
    Type your goal in plain language. For example:

    ```text
    "Launch a new marketing campaign for Q3"
    ```

    You don't need to use special syntax — write the way you'd explain the goal to a colleague.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the structured plan">
    Jetie AI responds with a hierarchical breakdown: top-level milestones, subtasks under each milestone, suggested owners (based on your workspace members), and time estimates. Read through it and decide what to keep or change.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit inline before saving">
    Each task in the AI response is editable directly in the chat panel. Click any task title, description, owner, or estimate to modify it before the plan touches your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the plan to your workspace">
    Click **Add to workspace** to create a Task database populated with all the generated tasks, milestones, and metadata. Jetie creates the database in your current space by default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign team members and adjust due dates">
    Once the Task database is created, open it to assign specific team members and set precise due dates. The AI's suggestions serve as a starting point — you're always in control of the final values.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Decomposition Examples

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Simple Goal">
    **Goal entered:** "Write and publish a blog post about our new feature"

    **AI-generated breakdown:**

    - **Milestone 1 — Research & Outline** *(1 day)*
      - Review feature release notes
      - Identify target audience and key message
      - Draft a 5-point outline

    - **Milestone 2 — Writing** *(2 days)*
      - Write first draft (~800 words)
      - Add screenshots or diagrams
      - Internal review pass

    - **Milestone 3 — Publishing** *(0.5 days)*
      - Copy-edit and proofread
      - Upload to CMS and set metadata
      - Schedule or publish post

    Simple goals produce a flat, focused plan — ideal for individual contributors working on a single deliverable.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Complex Project">
    **Goal entered:** "Launch a new marketing campaign for Q3 targeting enterprise customers in EMEA"

    **AI-generated breakdown:**

    - **Milestone 1 — Strategy & Positioning** *(1 week)*
      - Define ICP and messaging pillars
      - Competitive landscape review
      - Sign off on campaign brief

    - **Milestone 2 — Asset Production** *(2 weeks)*
      - Write long-form landing page copy
      - Produce 3-part email nurture sequence
      - Design social ad creatives (6 variants)
      - Record 60-second explainer video

    - **Milestone 3 — Channel Setup** *(1 week)*
      - Configure LinkedIn and Google Ads campaigns
      - Set up UTM tracking and attribution
      - QA all landing page flows

    - **Milestone 4 — Launch & Optimise** *(Ongoing)*
      - Go live on campaign start date
      - Weekly performance review
      - A/B test subject lines and ad copy

    Complex projects receive multi-level breakdowns with cross-functional ownership suggestions and realistic sequencing based on dependencies.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Refining the Plan

You don't have to accept the first decomposition. Use follow-up messages to iterate:

- **"Make this more granular"** — AI splits each task into smaller subtasks
- **"Simplify this"** — AI collapses the plan to the essential milestones only
- **"Add a QA phase"** — AI inserts a new milestone with relevant subtasks
- **"Assume a team of two"** — AI rebalances the workload accordingly

The AI retains the full context of the decomposition throughout your session, so each refinement builds on the last.

## Context-Aware Planning

If your workspace already contains pages or tasks related to your goal, Jetie automatically incorporates them. For example, if you have an existing **Q3 Roadmap** page, the AI references it when generating the breakdown — avoiding duplicate tasks and aligning milestones with your existing timeline.

## Autopilot Mode

Autopilot takes decomposition one step further. In addition to creating the Task database, the AI drafts the first page for each milestone and links everything together into a connected project structure — all before you've written a single word.

<Warning>
  Autopilot mode requires your explicit confirmation before making any changes to your workspace. A preview of all pages and tasks to be created is shown first. Nothing is saved until you click **Approve and create**. You can cancel or modify the plan at any point during the review.
</Warning>

## Exporting the Plan

Once your task database is created, you can share the plan with stakeholders who don't have Jetie access. Open the Task database, click the **⋯** menu in the top-right corner, and select **Export → PDF**. The exported PDF includes all milestones, subtasks, owners, and due dates in a clean, printable layout.
