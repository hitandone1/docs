Jetie brings your documents, knowledge base, and AI assistant together in one place, so you can stop switching between tools and start focusing on the work that matters. Whether you're drafting a product spec, managing a knowledge base, or breaking down a complex project, Jetie gives you everything you need in a single, cohesive workspace.

## What is Jetie?

Jetie is an AI-powered productivity workspace that unifies three capabilities that teams typically stitch together from separate tools:

- **Block-based document editing** — A Notion-style editor where every piece of content is a flexible, movable block. Write, structure, and reorganize your documents without fighting your tools.
- **PDF management** — Import, annotate, and export PDFs directly inside your workspace. No more bouncing between Confluence, Drive, and a PDF viewer.
- **AI chat and task decomposition** — A semi-autonomous AI assistant that answers questions in the context of your workspace and breaks down high-level goals into actionable, trackable steps.

Instead of juggling Notion for notes, Confluence for documentation, and a separate AI tool for ideation, Jetie gives you one environment where all three work together seamlessly.

## Who is Jetie For?

Jetie is designed for anyone who works with knowledge — whether that's a solo founder keeping their thinking organized or a cross-functional team managing documentation at scale.

- **Individuals and freelancers** — Keep personal notes, client docs, and project plans in one place without paying for multiple tools.
- **Product managers** — Write PRDs, decompose feature goals into tasks, and share living documents with engineering and design.
- **Developers** — Document APIs, track technical decisions, and use AI to scaffold specs and summaries directly in your workflow.
- **Knowledge workers** — Build and maintain a searchable knowledge base that your AI assistant can reference when answering questions.
- **Teams of all sizes** — Collaborate in real time on shared workspaces with role-based access, comment threads, and notification controls.

## Plans

Jetie offers a **free plan** that gives you access to the block editor, unlimited pages, PDF tools, and up to 50 AI interactions per month — no credit card required. Paid plans unlock unlimited AI usage, advanced permissions, version history, and priority support.

<Tip>
  You can start on the free plan and upgrade at any time from **Settings → Billing**. Your content and workspace structure are always preserved when you upgrade or downgrade.
</Tip>
