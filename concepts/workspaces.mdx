A workspace is the root of everything you do in Jetie. Every page you write, every task you track, and every document you upload lives inside a workspace. Whether you are working alone or collaborating with a large team, the workspace gives you a single, organized home for all your content.

## Personal vs. Team Workspaces

Jetie offers two distinct workspace types to match how you work.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Personal Workspace" icon="user">
    Owned by a single account. Ideal for personal notes, private projects, and individual task management. No other members can access it unless you explicitly invite them.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Team Workspace" icon="users">
    Shared among multiple members with role-based access controls. Built for collaborative projects, shared documentation, and cross-functional task tracking.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Workspace Hierarchy

Content inside a workspace follows a four-level hierarchy. Understanding this structure helps you keep large projects navigable and logically grouped.

```
Workspace
└── Section
    └── Page
        └── Sub-page
```

| Level | Description |
|---|---|
| **Workspace** | The top-level container. You switch between workspaces from the left sidebar. |
| **Section** | A labeled group of pages within the workspace sidebar — similar to a folder. |
| **Page** | An individual document containing blocks, databases, or embedded files. |
| **Sub-page** | A page nested inside another page, inheriting its parent's section context. |

<Tip>
  You can nest sub-pages as deeply as you need. For most teams, keeping hierarchies to three or four levels makes navigation easier.
</Tip>

## Switching Between Workspaces

You can belong to multiple workspaces at the same time. To switch between them:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the workspace switcher">
    Click your workspace name in the top-left corner of the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a workspace">
    All workspaces you belong to appear in the dropdown. Click any name to switch instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create or join a new workspace">
    Select **Add workspace** to create a fresh workspace, or paste an invite link to join an existing team workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Members and Roles

Every team workspace has a member roster visible under **Settings → Members**. When you invite someone, you assign them one of four roles.

### Role Permissions

| Permission | Owner | Admin | Member | Guest |
|---|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| Invite members | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Delete pages | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |
| Manage billing | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Edit content | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |

<Accordion title="Role descriptions">
  - **Owner** — The workspace creator. Has full control over all settings, billing, and membership. Each workspace has exactly one Owner.
  - **Admin** — Can manage members, configure workspace settings, and delete any page. Cannot access billing.
  - **Member** — Can create, edit, and delete their own pages and collaborate on shared pages.
  - **Guest** — Read-only access by default. Guests cannot edit content unless a Member or Admin explicitly grants page-level edit permissions.
</Accordion>

## Workspace Templates

When you create a new workspace, Jetie offers a set of curated templates to help you hit the ground running. Templates pre-populate your workspace with sections, pages, and task databases suited to common use cases.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Startup OS" icon="rocket">
    Roadmap, OKRs, meeting notes, and a hiring tracker — everything an early-stage team needs in one workspace.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Personal Productivity" icon="brain">
    Daily journal, weekly review, project backlog, and a reading list, all wired together for solo use.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Engineering Hub" icon="code">
    Sprint board, architecture decision records (ADRs), runbooks, and an on-call log.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Product Management" icon="map">
    Feature backlog, PRD templates, release calendar, and a feedback tracker.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Knowledge Base" icon="book-open">
    Structured sections for company policies, how-to guides, and onboarding docs.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Blank Workspace" icon="file">
    Start from scratch with an empty workspace and build your own structure.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  You can apply a template to an existing workspace as well. Go to **Settings → Templates** and select **Apply template** — Jetie will add the template's sections and pages without overwriting your existing content.
</Note>
